Why VisionDirector?

Real-world creative briefs are long, multi-objective, and tightly coupled. Existing diffusion models excel at aesthetics, but struggle to faithfully follow long instructions involving layout, typography, lighting, and semantics.

Real Designer Prompts

150–300 word briefs with 15–25 interdependent goals across composition, text, color, and lighting.

Existing Pipelines

One-shot prompting often misses local constraints and accumulates hallucinations across edits.

VisionDirector

Goal-aware planning, iterative refinement, and verification after every generation or edit.

What is VisionDirector?

VisionDirector is a closed-loop control system for image generation and editing. It introduces a director-style agent that reasons about goals, supervises edits, and verifies progress throughout the generation process.

Planner (VLM)

Decomposes long instructions into structured goals and decides when to generate, edit, verify, or stop.

Editor (Diffusion)

Executes focused visual updates without retraining high-quality backbone models.

Verifier (VLM Judge)

Evaluates goal satisfaction, provides structured feedback, and triggers rollback when regressions occur.

Closed-Loop Refinement

VisionDirector iteratively plans, edits, and verifies images until all goals are satisfied or no further improvements are possible.

Multi-step refinement with goal tracking, verification, and rollback.

Efficient Planning with GRPO

We further fine-tune the planner using Group Relative Policy Optimization (GRPO), teaching the director when to edit, verify, or stop.

GRPO reduces editing rounds by 26% while improving overall goal coverage, without retraining diffusion backbones.

LGBench: Long-Goal Benchmark

LGBench is a dual-modality benchmark designed to evaluate long-horizon instruction following in both text-to-image and image-to-image settings.

  • 2,000 tasks (1,000 T2I + 1,000 I2I)
  • 29,000+ annotated goals
  • Automated goal-level verification
